Photography has become quite an art in itself. Many people do it daily through cellphone cameras, instagram and through their personal camera. Everyone's perception on photography is different and to one's eyes a person may see the picture differently than the other. When taking photos, a photographer captures the image of what they see or what one wants to show on display or how to view one sees the picture as a whole. Some photographers may focus a lot on the subject or object and some may see things as a bigger and more broader spectrum as it may be. In any case, one can imagine or use their imagination to portray what a picture conveys to the audience, perhaps either a story-telling or just a beautiful capture of what one sees in their own perspective and perception.
